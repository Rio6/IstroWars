IstroWars

Stars

The star names are from old server names, plus a bunch of auto generated names. They are connected to each other for players and factions to move.

Factions

The faction list is retrieved from IstroStats. Each faction has an influence value on each star. The influence changes based on the games players in the star play. A faction expands into nearby star when its influence reaches 80%, but if it drops below 5%, it gets kicked from the star.

Players

Each day, a player can move to a nearby star. They can also change their faction tag in game at any time. Their location and faction determines the influence change of local factions when they play games.

Influence

When a game ends and all of these conditions are met:
  • The game mode is 1v1, 1v1r, 1v1t, 2v2, or 3v3
  • There is a winning team
  • All players on the winning team are in the same star
then the influence of each faction on the star would change. For each faction in the star, their influence is increased by 1% for each player in the winning team who wears its tag. If there are no player in the winning team that wears its tag, the influence is deccreased by 1% instead.
